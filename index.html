just a bit about us
Hello and welcome. We're so glad you're here. We started this creative business out of love for all things natural, handmade, and refined. We noticed that the majority of the invitations in our region were heavy on overcomplicated typography and rigid designs. We wanted to create Fine Art Wedding Invitations for the bride who is very aesthetically driven, prefers minimalism over maximalism, and who does not settle for the ordinary.
